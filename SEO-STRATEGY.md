# Speak Arizona — SEO Strategy & Growth Plan

## Overview
Speak Arizona is a weekly podcast about public speaking, leadership, and communication hosted by Rupesh Parbhoo. Powered by District 3 Toastmasters (Arizona, New Mexico, West Texas).

**Target Keywords (Long-term):**
- "public speaking podcast"
- "best public speaking podcasts"
- "communication skills podcast"

**Defensible Niche:** Toastmasters credibility + Arizona local focus + workplace communication for everyday professionals.

## Phase 4: Compete Head-On (Month 12+)

### Requirements to Rank for Head Keywords
- 150+ indexed pages
- 50+ backlinks from unique domains
- Consistent weekly content publishing
- Strong topical authority signals
- Engagement metrics (time on site, repeat visits, low bounce rate)

### Strategy
- [ ] Target head keywords with dedicated pillar pages
- [ ] Create non-episode content competitors don't make (guides, tools, checklists, templates)
- [ ] Build topical clusters: each pillar page supported by 10+ related blog posts
- [ ] Outrank listicle sites by being both the content AND the podcast

---

## The Secret Weapon
Our competitors are podcasts with websites. We're building a website with a podcast. That means we can create non-episode content — guides, tools, checklists, blog posts — that podcast-first competitors never will.
